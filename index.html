Questions & Answers

Honest answers to the questions on your mind.

Is IVF painful?+
Mild discomfort — but very manageable. Egg retrieval is done under sedation. Most patients say it was "much easier than expected."
What are our realistic chances of success?+
At your free consultation, we give you a personalised assessment — not a headline number. Our clinical pregnancy rate is among the highest in the region.
We've had failed cycles before. Is there still hope?+
Yes. A failed cycle is data, not a final answer. Our recurrent failure specialists will review your history and look for what was missed.
How much does treatment cost?+
Fully discussed at consultation. We offer flexible payment plans and work with most major UAE insurers. The initial consultation is free.
How soon can we get an appointment?+
Typically within 3–5 working days. Same-week appointments available for urgent cases.
